Delicious Vanilla Cupcakes

A simple recipe for soft, fluffy cupcakes

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ cup milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Line a muffin tray with cupcake liners.
  2. In a bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, then mix in vanilla.
  5. Gradually add dry ingredients, alternating with milk.
  6. Fill cupcake liners 2/3 full and bake for 18–20 minutes.
  7. Cool completely before frosting.
